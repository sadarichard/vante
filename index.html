Frequently Asked
Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQs) by users

Is this platform confidential and anonymous?
Yes. We prioritize user privacy and security. All communication and information are protected by industry-standard security measures.
Is there a cost to use the platform?
We provide free resources such as articles, self-help tools and support groups. For professional consultations and therapy sessions, we offer payment per sessions.
Do I need insurance to use the platform?
Insurance is not required to use our platform. We understand that accessing mental health support can be a challenge and we strive to make our services accessible to everyone.
How do I schedule an appointment?
Scheduling an appointment is easy! Once yo've created an account on our platform, you can browse through available therapists, read their profiles and select the one that best suits your needs.
What kind of resources are available?
Our platform offers a variety of resources to support your mental well-being. You can access:
  • Informative articles on various mental health topics
  • Self-help tools and exercises
  • Support groups to connect with others facing similar challenges
  • Professional consultations with therapists and counsellors
  • Online therapy sessions for added convenience
