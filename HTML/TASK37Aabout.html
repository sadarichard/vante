WELCOME TO GOD'S KINGDOM SOCIETY
YOUTH FELLOWSHIP, SAPELE BRANCH

BRIEF OVERVIEW

The GKS Youth Fellowship Sapele Branch was inaugurated in 1979 with Late Brother (Minister) Moses Owivri as first Youth Chairman and Late Brother (Prof.) Daniel Ilega as Secretary. Late Brother (Minister) Moses Owivri and his Committee piloted the Youth activities for a considerable number of years after which Late Brother David Okah was appointed Youth Chairman.

In 1984, another Youth Fellowship Committee took over having Brother C.C Ogirigbo as Youth Chairman and Brother J.E Ivwighreghweta as Youth Secretary among other officers. They did oversee the affairs of the fellowship till the year 1991.

Brother Michael Bazunu was elected Youth Chairman in 1991 and served for two tenures on his re-election that took him from 1991-2000. Other members of his Committee include, Brothers Festus A. Eyekomiogban (V. Chairman), Joel Omare (Secretary), Late J.F.K Adibor (Assist. Secretary), Israel Evueh (Financial Secretary), Late Johnson Ike (Treasurer), Martins Oguashi (PRO) with Brothers Matthew Igwe and Jolly Adjarho as members.

In the year 2000, Brother Festus A. Eyekomiogban was elected Youth Chairman. Other elected officers who served from 2000-2003 include: Brother Michael Umukoro (V. Chairman), Paul Evueh (Secretary), Moses Mogboyiteren (Assist. Secretary), Ebi Assoh (Financial Secretary), Matthew Igwe (Treasurer) and Godstime Egbedi (PRO).

Brother Paul Evueh became the next Youth Chairman upon his election in the year 2003. He was re-elected in 2006 and served till 2009. Other members of the Committee are: Brothers Paul Iguele (V. Chairman), Mogboyiteren (Secretary), Late Bro. Francis Esiebo, (Assist. Secretary), Egbo Christopher (Fin. Secretary), Kefe Ayonuwe (Treasurer).

From 2009-2012, we had the following and they include late Bro. Esiebo Francis (Chairman), Egbo Christopher (V.Chairman), Otaghogho Evueh (Secretary), Aghogho Erivwo (Ass. Secretary), Samuel Adiawa (Treasurer), Ojulu Matthew (Fin. Sec), Brother Jebba Prince (Pub. Sec). Upon relocation of Bro. Otaghogho Evueh to warri in 2010/11, Bro. Erivwo Aghogho was made secretary while Bro. Daniel was co-opted and made Ass. Secretary.

The following officers emerged winners during the election of 2012 and they served from 2012-2015. They are Brothers, Egbo Christopher (Chairman), Onoriode Ayonuwe (V. Chairman), Daniel Evueh (Secretary), Matthew Ojulu (Assist. Secretary), Justice Obotuke (Treasurer), Samuel Adiawa (Fin. Sec.), Prince Jebba (PRO), Late Bro. Francis Esiebo and Aghogho Erivwo (Ex-officio) and Efajero Ivwighreghweta (member).

The following officers emerged winners in 2015 and they served from 2015-2018. They are: Brothers Daniel Evueh (Chairman), Onoriode Ayonuwe (V. Chairman), Aghogho Erivwo (Secretary), Oghenekevwe E. Iyeke (Assist. Secretary), Samuel Adiawa (Fin. Secretary), Benjamin Ovuakporaye (Treasurer), Benedict Ajogri (Pub. Secretary), Prince Jebba (Ex-Officio), Justice Obotuke (Ex-Officio), Atuyota Uchefe (Member/Zonal Coordinator) and Okpako Akpotojor (Member). However, when Bro. Benjamin Ovuoporaye relocated to Rivers State in 2016, the Committee notified the Administrative Committee and unanimously agreed that Bro. Prince Jebba take his position as Treasurer while Okpako Akpojotor was co-opted as member.

The following officers emerged winners from 2018-2021 include Brothers Daniel Evueh (Chairman), Aghogho Erivwo (V. Chairman), Samuel Adiawa (Secretary), Oghenekevwe E. Iyeke (Assist. Secretary), Bright Omorogbe (Treasurer), Onoriode Ayonuwe (Fin. Secretary), Ason Michael (Publicity Sec.), Atuyota Uchefe (Zonal Coordinator), Awheyevu Victor (Member) and Ayonuwe Kefe (Member).

In 2021, the following were to serve till 2024. They include: Brothers Egbedi Godstime as (Chairman), Ahanor Osazee (V. Chairman), Ese Sada-Okpaka (Secretary), Evueh Ojiyovwi Benjamin (Asst. Secretary), Emmanuel Ogirigbo (Fin. Secretary), Bright Omorogbe (Treasurer), Micheal Ason (PRO), Evueh Daniel (Zonal Coordinator), Samuel Adiawa (Ex- Officio) and Ovwie Christopher as (Member). However, when Bro. Godstime Egbedi relocated to Eku in 2022, the Committee notified the Administrative Committee and a bye election was done and Bro. Ese Okpaka emerged as Chairman, Bro Michael Ason emerged as Secretary while Bro Bazunu Gamaliel became the Publicity Secretary.

In 2024, the following were elected to serve till 2027. The are brothers Ese Sada-Okpaka (Chairman), Osazee Ahanor (Vice Chairman), Michael Ason (Secretary), Ediri Ogirigbo (Ass. Secretary), Job Great (Fin. Secretary), Emmanuel Ogirigbo (Treasurer), Benjamin Bazunu (P.R.O), Oyawiri Emuobosa (Member), Godstime Ovwie (Ex- Officio) and Bright Omorogbe (Ex-Officio)
